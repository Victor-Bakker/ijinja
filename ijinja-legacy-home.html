Our Story

The Spirit of Ij!nja

Long ago, in the rolling hills where the Zulu and Xhosa people gathered to share stories by the fire, there grew a root with a fiery spirit — Ij!nja, the ginger root. Revered for its sharp bite and restorative warmth, Ij!nja was more than a flavour; it was a symbol of vitality and strength.

Crafted with care and respect for tradition, the spirit of Ij!nja was reborn.  Ij!nja is not merely a drink — it is the taste of Nguni roots, a celebration of warmth and fire, of laughter and connection, and of moments best shared together.
